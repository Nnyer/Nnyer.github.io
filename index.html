About Me
I am a romantic. I mean that in both the expression of love, and the starry-eyed feeling that you get when you look at the moon in a sea of darkness that makes it seem so bright. It should come as no surprise then, that I like to think and daydream a lot. It is fun to be able to look at the world in this kind of way. Though, at times, it's a trait that is undesirable, such as in times when you are at your lowest and you start thinking about the darkest of thoughts. Let's just say that dark thoughts and a brain like this don't really mix all that well. In addition to that, Whenever I achieve something great or I think of myself as anything but the most vile being imaginable. It is always complimented with a generous glass of self-loathing. I am someone that deserves nothing but the worst.

I would say that I am someone who moulds into situation like sand and water. Wherein it's shape depends on the vessel that it resides in. For example, if I were in a group of people that spoke alot, I would talk proportionally less than those around me. Flip it around and make the group quiet, I'd take center stage and keep talking, or... maybe not. It's entirely dependent on the specific situation that I am in. Like Frankenstein's monster, patched up with all kinds of different flesh, and experiences that I have encountered. I have so much stitches on me that you'd start to wonder who I trully am. Which piece of flesh am I? Like the ship of Theseus, what defines this monster? With that said, I can fit into a variety of things no matter how awkwardly... Since I am pretty much a piece of everything, the good parts and the bad. I am both someone who can fit into everything, as well as someone who cannot fit anywhere. No one will ever love me and hate me more than myself.

I remember this one well, it was the first in what I would call the 2nd Phase of this kind of artwork. I say that because if you noticed, it is drawn on a folded piece of A4 paper, instead of having it be on the back of my notebook.
Disconnect: The second part of the 2nd phase . I made this when I was in a very weird part of my life, I felt that every feeling, friendship, thoughts, sense of self that I had were disconnected, as if there were parts that stand on their own, never connecting to the other parts of me. as an example: I like ice cream but hate it when it has nuts in it but I would still eat it if I was feeling kind of down but not the kind of down that I would want to think the whole day, but sometimes I would want to eat it after thinking the whole day but but and or but and or but...
Bleed: The third part of the 2nd Phase . In this one, I wanted to have a theme around it this time instead of just throwing random things at it and trying to make it somewheat cohesive. Not much to it other than, I tried to make it flow downwards, as if it was bleeding.
Fear: The fourth or fifth part of the 2nd Phase . I wanted to portray what fear felt like. the fear of being judged, trying something new, committing to a big decision. I wanted to convey how little it make you feel, or rather, how overbearing these fears are. Not only does the person in the middle seem smaller than the manifestation of fear that surround them, the little dot in the middle conveys that they feel so much smaller than they really are, but also, I found it interesting that the bigger the fear, the smaller they will feel themselves to be. It's that contrast that I thought was an interesting dynamic and wanted to put it on paper.

For some reason or another, I really like to draw cursed things... At first I did it because it was edgy, but I kinda grew to actually like drawing them. Then I found that Mumei from hololive also does the same thing. Though I was already fine with the situation, it kinda gave me solace that someone else shared the same thoughts as me. Kind of connects to the whole frankenstein's monster thing. I saw a piece of me, that will be hard to understand for other people, in another person.
